Our Story

More Than Just Coffee

Nestled in the heart of East Barnet Village, Bean Cafe Bistro is your neighbourhood escape from the everyday. We're a family-friendly, independent cafe and bistro where the coffee is always freshly ground, the food is made with love, and every guest is treated like family.

Whether you're popping in for a quick morning espresso, settling in for a lazy weekend brunch, or grabbing a homemade cake to take home — we've got you covered. Perfect for solo dining, catching up with friends, or bringing the kids.
